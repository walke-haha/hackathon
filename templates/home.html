CONTACT@IIIT

The contact directory designed by three IIIT-H students is an efficient and user-friendly platform that enables users to search and manage the information of students and faculty members. With its robust search feature, users can easily locate students and faculty members by entering relevant information. The platform also allows users to add new students or faculty members to the directory and edit their information as needed. This tool is a valuable asset to any educational institution or organization, providing an organized and accessible database of contact information.
